Ok man, heres the general counters:

Terran:
Goliath: Goliath with missle iupgrade can shoot very far, so they work very well.
Battle Cruisers: Meh they work Yamato works very well.

Zerg:
Hydralisks: Mass hydralisks will rape carriers. Also put dark swarm over them so the carriers cant hit you.
Devourers: They work, but cost a little too much.

Protoss:
Dragoons: Dragoons have a nice attack to kill carriers. Make sure you have the dragoon range upgrade.

High Templar: Hightemplar are by far the best counter for carriers, and also battle cruisers. You can psi-storm a group of them afew times and they will fall very fast. Suggest 6 or more high templar with the mana upgrade so u can cast it lots and lots of times. Also have some goons to pick off the strays =].


Adittional advice: When using Goliath, Dragoons or Hydralisks, make sure
YOU TARGET THE ACTUAL CARRIER and not the interceptors. The carriers themselves arent that strong, so focus your fire on the carriers to take them down.

If you've teched to Defilers, you can burrow them some distance away from your base. Have a zergling or someting cheap a good distance in front of the Defiler. That way when he sends his Carriers, you'll see ahead of time, and that way if he has detection, your defiler wont be the first target. Then unburrow and plague his carriers and, if you can, retreat to your base and prepare for Dark swarm. But I personally think the PLague is enough to discourage him from using carriers.
